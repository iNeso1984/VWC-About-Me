Web Developer and Designer

Vanessa Withun

The visual arts has always been a part of my background. I have been painting and drawing for over six years and have appeared in exhibitions throughout the United States. I am passionate about designing websites that are attractive and user-friendly. I decided to enter into the world of web development and coding in order to use what I have learned as a visual artists with a military background and a background in science to explore new design concepts, such as graphic design, animation, and virtual reality. I am currently studying web design at Sessions College for Professional Design. My goal is to become a Full-Stack Web Developer and Designer in order to create dynamic websites that are visually compelling for the user, whether the user is shopping or browsing a company's website.
